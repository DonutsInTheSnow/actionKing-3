Pipe Efficiency

Over the years debris builds up on pipes creating a blockage over time that leads to clogs and stoppages. Make sure your pipes are working efficiently with Action King Services. Our professional drain cleaning keeps your pipes in excellent condition and everything running smoothly.

Professional Drain Cleaning

At Action King, our approach is not just about unclogging drains, it's about ensuring that your drainage systems operate at peak efficiency, preventing future issues before they arise.

Our team of skilled technicians employs the latest in drain cleaning technology to tackle any blockage, no matter how stubborn. From grease build-up in restaurant kitchens to tree roots invading residential sewer lines, we have the expertise and equipment to clear it efficiently and effectively. Our services include high-pressure water jetting, which not only removes blockages but also cleans the pipes, reducing the likelihood of future clogs.

We understand that drain issues can be more than just an inconvenience; they can lead to significant health and safety hazards, not to mention costly repairs if left unchecked. Our commitment extends beyond the immediate fix. We provide advice on maintaining your drainage system to prevent future problems, saving you time and money in the long run.

With Action King, your drains are in capable hands, ready to handle everything from routine maintenance to emergency interventions.
