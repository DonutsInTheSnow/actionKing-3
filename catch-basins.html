Maintained Catch Basins Can Last For Years

Catch basin cleaning is an essential service for maintaining sewer systems, preventing costly storm sewer blockages, and protecting the community's water supply. At Action King, we specialize in providing timely and professional catch basin cleaning services for municipalities, management companies, and contractors.

Catch basins are vital components of storm drainage systems. These structures, typically found in streets, have a metal grate that traps debris and a sump that collects sediment. By removing large debris like leaves, sand, and rocks, we help prevent clogs that could lead to flooding and damage to surrounding areas. Our specialized vacuum equipment ensures thorough cleaning, maintaining the integrity and efficiency of your storm sewer system.

Regular catch basin cleaning helps avoid issues such as flooding, pest infestations, and structural damage. Neglected basins can attract pests, breed mosquitoes, and even cause sinkholes if the base erodes. By keeping your catch basins clean and well-maintained, we reduce the risk of these problems, ensuring optimal drainage and preserving your property's landscape.

Action King's catch basin service is an essential part of maintaining effective drainage systems, safeguarding your property from potential damage, and keeping stormwater runoff under control. Ensure your catch basins are cleaned regularly to prevent blockages, preserve water quality, and avoid expensive repairs.
