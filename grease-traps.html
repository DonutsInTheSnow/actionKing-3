Catering To Kitchen Sizes

At Action King Services, we understand the critical role grease traps play in every commercial kitchen. These traps are not just about maintaining cleanliness; they're about ensuring a healthy environment and enhancing equipment efficiency. Every cooking process generates waste—fats, oils, and grease—that must be managed responsibly. Our service at Action King involves the professional pumping and cleaning of both interior kitchen grease traps and exterior grease tanks, catering to sizes from the modest 500 gallons up to the massive 25,000 gallons.

Keep Your Kitchen Operating At Its Peak

Why does this matter? Beyond the obvious benefits of cleanliness and safety, well-maintained grease traps significantly boost the longevity and performance of kitchen equipment. Clogged or poorly maintained grease traps can lead to equipment breakdowns, reduced efficiency, and even fire hazards. Our approach at Action King isn't just about removal; it's about ensuring your kitchen operates at its peak, free from the risks associated with grease accumulation.

Our fleet, featuring grease vacuum trucks with capacities of 2000 and 6000 gallons, is tailored to handle these tasks with precision and care. We're not just providing a service; we're offering peace of mind, ensuring that your kitchen environment remains safe, compliant, and efficient. With Action King, you're choosing a partner dedicated to the health and functionality of your commercial kitchen, combining technology, expertise, and a commitment to sustainability.
